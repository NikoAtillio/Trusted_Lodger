About Trusted Lodger

Discover Trusted Lodger, the UK-based online platform that redefines homesharing by connecting lodgers with homesharers in a truly innovative way. With our AI-powered matching system, we pair individuals based on personality types and lifestyle preferences, ensuring harmonious living situations right from the start. Our unique approach not only saves time and money but also removes the anxiety of moving in with incompatible housemates.


Whether you're a young professional, a student, or a senior seeking intergenerational living arrangements, Trusted Lodger offers a diverse range of options to suit your needs. Join us and experience the peace of mind that comes with finding like-minded individuals and creating a comfortable, welcoming living environment.

How Does It Work?

Step 1

Create Your Profile

Start by creating a profile that highlights who you are and what you’re looking for. Whether you’re a lodger searching for a cozy space or a landlord offering a welcoming home, this step ensures we can help you connect with the right match.

Step 2

Search & Find Your Match

Browse through a wide variety of listings tailored to your needs. Use filters to narrow down results by location, budget, or preferences to find homes or lodgers that align with your lifestyle and personality.

Step 3

Match & Connect

Our AI-powered matching system pairs you with compatible housemates or lodgers based on your preferences. Once you find a match, connect with them to discuss further details and arrange a viewing or move-in date.

It's that easy!
