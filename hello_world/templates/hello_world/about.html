Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What exactly is a live-in landlord?

Simply put, a 'live-in landlord' or 'resident landlord', is someone that rents out part of their home, while also living in the property.

What is Trusted Lodger?

Trusted Lodger is an online platform that connects lodgers with live-in landlords across the UK. We provide a secure, easy, and affordable way to find a place to call home while fostering meaningful connections and creating welcoming living spaces.

What makes Trusted Lodger different?

Unlike other platforms, Trusted Lodger focuses on smaller, more personal living arrangements—typically a live-in landlord with one or two lodgers. We prioritize compatibility, trust, and meaningful connections while offering added services like background checks and secure payments to make renting stress-free.

For Landlords

As a homeowner, do I need permission to let out part of my house?

If you own the property outright, no special permissions are needed. If you have an outstanding mortgage, you need to have a quick check in with your lender. Most mortgage agreements allow you to take in a lodger as long as you live in the property.

Why should I use Trusted Lodger?

Trusted Lodger helps you easily rent out your spare room while ensuring safety and simplicity. Whether you're looking for extra income, companionship, or a bit of help around the house, we provide a secure platform that takes care of everything from finding the right lodger to managing rent payments.

How much does it cost to list my room?

We offer a free tier where you can list one room, get basic visibility, and receive one free monthly boost. If you want to attract more interest, our paid plans start at £12.95 for a two-week upgrade and go up to £45.95 per month for premium features like weekly boosts, premium tools, and priority support.

For Lodgers

How does Trusted Lodger work for lodgers?

It's simple! Sign up, create a profile, and start browsing available rooms that match your lifestyle and preferences. You can contact landlords directly through the platform and arrange a viewing or chat to find your perfect match.

Is it free to use Trusted Lodger as a lodger?

Yes, there's a free tier that lets you view up to 10 profiles per day and contact up to two matches each week. If you'd like unlimited access and advanced features like profile boosts and filters, you can upgrade to one of our paid plans starting at £9.95 for one week.

I don't own my house. Can a lodger live with me?

If you are a secure council tenant, you are allowed to sublet part of the property - as long as you have the council's permission first! Private tenants are generally prohibited from subletting but checking your contract or chatting to your landlord will be able to clarify this for you.
